Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ASUS ROG Zephyrus G16?

The ASUS ROG Zephyrus G16 is a high-performance gaming laptop known for its powerful hardware, sleek design, and premium display. It’s designed for gamers, content creators, and power users who need a balance of portability and performance.

Does the laptop overheat while gaming?

The Zephyrus G16 features ROG Intelligent Cooling, which includes a vapor chamber, liquid metal cooling, and multiple fan modes to optimize temperature. However, high-performance gaming may still cause the laptop to get warm.

How long does the battery last?

  • Gaming: 2-4 hours (depending on settings)
  • Normal use: 6-8 hours (web browsing, video playback)
  • Eco mode: Can extend battery life further
